# 2026 CHAOSS Governing Board Election Results

We are happy to announce the six newly elected members of the CHAOSS Governing Board, each serving a two-year term:

- **Adeyinka Oresanya** (Independent)
- **Cali Dolfi** (Red Hat)
- **Divya Mohan** (SUSE)
- **Harmony Elendu** (Independent)
- **Peculiar C. Umeh** (Superbloom)
- **Victoria Ottah** (Accessibility Nigeria)

Please take a moment to congratulate them!

The competition was strong with 8 candidates running for 6 seats, so thank you to everyone who ran in this election and everyone who took the time to cast their ballot!

We thank all candidates for their willingness to serve and their contributions to the CHAOSS community.

Cheers,
