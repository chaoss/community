## My Background 
I am a Technical Project and Product Manager whose work sits at the intersection of documentation, program design, and community building. For over three years, I have contributed to CHAOSS through project management, technical documentation, and program coordination with a consistent focus on making the community more accessible, sustainable, and impactful both in Africa and globally.

Today, I lead the CHAOSS Technical Writers Focus Group, produceand host CHAOSScast, badge events and projects under the Badging initiative, and contribute to program management across the CHAOSS ecosystem. My work is grounded in a genuine belief that healthy communities require not just good metrics but also great documentation, clear governance, and contributors who feel truly supported.

## Why I'm Running
CHAOSS has always been about measuring what matters in open source communities and its ecosystem. I believe the next chapter of CHAOSS should go further, ensuring that the contributors who power our community are themselves healthy, well supported, and on a clear pathway to meaningful participation and leadership globally.

Over the next two years, my vision for CHAOSS centers on three priorities:
1. Contributor Health and Sustainability: Enabling frameworks and education programmes that keep contributors engaged, recognised, and supported, not just tracked.
2. Project Health and Participation Pathways: Making it easier for new and experienced contributors alike to thrive as CHAOSS governance grows and adapts in line with evolving needs - whether due to changes in technology or revisions in policy.
3. Inclusive Global Growth: Drawing on my experience building community and organising programs across Africa and globally, I will advocate for governance practices that reflect the full diversity of the CHAOSS ecosystem.

I will bring to the Board a practitioner's perspective someone who has navigated CHAOSS as a contributor, a program leader, a podcast host, and a documentation steward. I will leverage my experience to identify and smooth out all frictions from the inside while participating directly to help build the structures that will elevate contributors’ health which will build a healthier community for us.
