## My Contributions to CHAOSS

I joined CHAOSS in September 2023 and have participated in several working groups, including facilitating meetings for both CHAOSS and CHAOSS Africa. I initiated and co-led the CHAOSS Education Working Group, and led the development of the CHAOSS Education courses from ideation to production, including reviewing PRs, recording and editing all videos, reviewing scripts and slides, coordinating materials, and deplyoing all courses to the [Moodle platform (uploading videos and documentation)](https://chaoss.moodlecloud.com/). As part of the CHAOSS Education work, I mentored and guided several contributors to make their first PRs and engage in the community.

I co-led the redesign of CHAOSS metrics into a new template across five repositories: wg-dei, wg-metrics-development, wg-evolution, wg-value, and wg-risk as a way to learn more about the CHAOSS project as a whole. 
I have also contributed directly to several other CHAOSS efforts and was the primary author for the [CHAOSS Practitioner Guide on Building Diverse Leadership](https://chaoss.community/practitioner-guide-diverse-leadership) as part of the Data Science Working Group. I have also reviewed several [DEI badging applications](https://github.com/badging/event-diversity-and-inclusion/issues?q=is:issue+assignee:peculiaruc+is:closed). 
I served on the organizing committee for the CHAOSS EU Conference in 2024 and 2025, and was a CFP reviewer in 2026. Additionally, I facilites some CHAOSS Africa biweekly calls,worked as part of the CHAOSSCon Africa 2025 Committee and have helped in organizing the Disability and Inclusion Outreach.

## Why I'm Running

I am committed to strengthening CHAOSS through education, sustainability, and inclusivity. As the lead for the CHAOSS Education project, I will continue empowering newcomers and others with the knowledge and confidence to contribute meaningfully to open source via the CHAOSS educational resources. My goal is not only to develop courses but also to expand their reach, growing our audience from hundreds to thousands.

With over five years of open source experience, I bring both practical expertise and a vision for more structured operations. By treating education as infrastructure, expanding mentorship and sponsorship pipelines, I aim to secure a sustainable, inclusive future for the community, one where contributors thrive. I will also focus on (a) Contributors Growth. (b) Project Health and  (c) Project Transitioning: Improve CHAOSS governance to include a clear pathway for project transition in and out of CHAOSS.
