## My Contributions to CHAOSS

I have been actively participating in CHAOSS since July 2025 with my primary contributions being made under the Augur project, such as:
- participating in weekly meetings, including the working group public call and the weekly maintainers call
- Reviewing PRs submitted by my fellow maintainers as well as newcomers to the community
- Developing and testing new features and bug fixes
- Co-leading the Augur room at CHAOSScon EU 2026 in Brussels with several other Augur maintainers

Over these past few months, I have also helped set up and operate the livestream for CHAOSScon EU, led the creation of a new [AI disclosure detection and measurement tool](https://github.com/chaoss/ai-detection-action/), and participated in discussions in the AI alignment working group. In the past few weeks I have also helped create both a CHAOSS software collaboraton slack channel and a public channel for the new community infrastructure team.


## Why I'm Running

I hope to continue my participation in CHAOSS software and metrics tooling. With a seat on the board, I hope to be able to participate in moving the CHAOSS community forward, such as: finding new and diverse sources of funding that align with our community values, thinking of new ways to promote CHAOSS to different audiences, improving our cross-collaboration with other organizations, as well as helping organize and document our  community governance and strategy so we can be prepared for future first-time board members in next year's election and beyond. As a board member, I would be bringing a total of 13 years of experience in Open Source and community work, including both self-taught/self-practice and formal education in contributor and leader roles. I would also be bringing my own personal interests in [human/community-oriented and systems thinking](https://www.adriancedwards.com/blog/2026/thirteen-years-of-open-work) and a desire to help improve the CHAOSS lottery factor by empowering more community members to make their first contributions and stick around.
